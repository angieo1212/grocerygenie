✦ Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your Genie gets to work.

Which stores does GroceryGenie support? +
We currently support Kroger, Walmart, H-E-B, Aldi, Target, Publix, Safeway, Costco, Whole Foods, and 40+ more regional chains. We add new stores every month based on user requests.
Is my loyalty account information safe? +
Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and never store your passwords. We use secure, read-only connections to clip coupons on your behalf — your accounts are always protected.
How does auto coupon clipping work? +
Once you link your store loyalty accounts (like your Kroger Plus or Safeway card), GroceryGenie scans all available digital coupons and clips every one that matches an item on your list — automatically, before you even head to the store.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime with one click from your account dashboard. You keep full access until the end of your billing period, and we offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked.
Does GroceryGenie work for online grocery orders too? +
Yes! GroceryGenie works for both in-store shopping and online/pickup orders. We compare pickup and delivery prices so whether you shop in person or online, you always get the best deal.
