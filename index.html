Frequently Asked Questions

Which stores does GroceryGenie support?
We currently support Kroger, Walmart, H-E-B, Aldi, Target, Publix, Safeway, Costco, Whole Foods, and 40+ more regional chains. We're adding new stores every month.
Is my loyalty account information safe?
Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption and never store your passwords. We use secure read-only connections to clip coupons on your behalf.
How does auto coupon clipping work?
Once you link your store loyalty accounts (like your Kroger Plus card), GroceryGenie scans available digital coupons and clips every one that matches items on your list — automatically, before you shop.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime with one click. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, and we offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked.
Does GroceryGenie work for online grocery orders too?
Yes! GroceryGenie works for both in-store shopping and online/delivery orders. We compare pickup and delivery prices too so you always get the best deal.
